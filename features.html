Features

Here's a brief overview of what features you get with Shift.

Shift comes with a set of powerful moderation tools to punish the bad guys in your server. These include a striking system, an agree system, and a ban and kick system.

There's also a large suite of fun commands, which will bring a spark of joy to the channels in your server. Some of these commands include a hug command, a pizza command, and an alive command.

Finally, Shift has a few tool commands. These can simply make your life easier. There currently isn't as many utility commands as the other two categories, but more will be arriving very soon.
